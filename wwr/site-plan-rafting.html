Overview

Purpose

This website is inteded to bring in additional business for the White Water Rafting company located in Idaho Falls, Idaho. Potential clients can view testimonials, trip packages, maps, bio's of guides, and even schedules for when rafting is available. Reservations can also be made, for individals, groups, or company events. Purchasing merchandise is also available and can be shipped directly to your home or business. Most questions can be answered through the website, and if there is anything additional that you would like to know, please call us at (123) 456-7890.

Audience

This website is intended for the outdoor adventurer. If you like fast paced, adrenaline packed watersports, we have a package for you. If you prefer to be on the slower side and take it easy, but still enjoy a day on the river, we have packages to accomadate you as well. Any individual, or group is welcome, we only ask that you attend our safety class and abide by the rules. Due to insurance attendees must also pass a health screening and be at least 12 years old. If the participant is a minor a parent or guardian is required to accompany them.
